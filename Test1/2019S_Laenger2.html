Satz 4.53 (Limesform des Quotientenkriteriums): Aus lim sup⁡n→∞∣an+1an∣<1\displaystyle \limsup_{n \to \infty} \left|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right| < 1n→∞limsup​∣∣​an​an+1​​∣∣​<1 folgt die absolute Konvergenz der Reihe ∑nan\displaystyle \sum_n a_nn∑​an​ und aus lim sup⁡n→∞∣an+1an∣>1\displaystyle \limsup_{n \to \infty} \left|\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_n}\right| > 1n→∞limsup​∣∣​an​an+1​​∣∣​>1 deren Divergenz.

(Achtung Folge; keine Reihe)

Sandwich-Theorem

∑k=1nn2+kn4+k\displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n \sqrt{\frac{n^2+k}{n^4+k}}k=1∑n​n4+kn2+k​​

kleiner:

∑k=1nn2n4+n=n⋅n2n4+n=n2⋅1n21+1n3=11+1n3\displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n \sqrt{\frac{n^2}{n^4+n}}=n \cdot \sqrt{\frac{n^2}{n^4+n}}= \sqrt{\cancel{n^2}\cdot \frac{\frac{1}{\cancel{n^2}}}{1+\frac{1}{n^3}}}=\sqrt{\frac{1}{1+\frac{1}{n^3}}}k=1∑n​n4+nn2​​=n⋅n4+nn2​​=n2⋅1+n31​n21​​​=1+n31​1​​

lim⁡n→∞11+1n3=1\displaystyle \lim_{n \to \infin} \sqrt{\frac{1}{1+\frac{1}{n^3}}}=1n→∞lim​1+n31​1​​=1

größer:

∑k=1nn2+nn4=n⋅n2+nn4=n2⋅1n2+1n31=1+1n\displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n \sqrt{\frac{n^2+n}{n^4}}=n\cdot\sqrt{\frac{n^2+n}{n^4}}=\sqrt{\cancel{n^2}\cdot\frac{\frac{1}{\cancel{n^2}}+\frac{1}{\cancel{n^3}}}{1}}=\sqrt{1+\frac{1}{n}}k=1∑n​n4n2+n​​=n⋅n4n2+n​​=n2⋅1n21​+n31​​​=1+n1​​

lim⁡n→∞1+1n=1\displaystyle \lim_{n \to \infin} \sqrt{1+\frac{1}{n}}=1n→∞lim​1+n1​​=1

  ⟹  lim⁡n→∞∑k=1nn2+kn4+k=1\displaystyle \implies \lim_{n \to \infin} \sum_{k=1}^n \sqrt{\frac{n^2+k}{n^4+k}} =1⟹n→∞lim​k=1∑n​n4+kn2+k​​=1
